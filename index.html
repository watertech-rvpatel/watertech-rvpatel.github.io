About Me

I am a researcher specializing in membrane distillation, high-salinity wastewater treatment, and sustainable water recovery systems. My work integrates computational fluid dynamics (CFD), advanced materials, and experimental system design to address global water challenges.

Research Areas

  • Nanofiber and Mixed Matrix Membranes
  • Renewable Energy-based Water Treatment
